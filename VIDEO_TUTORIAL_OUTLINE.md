# Video Tutorial Feature Outline for Project StableKraft

## Overview Video - 5 Bullet Points

- **Discover and stream music from RSS feeds** - Browse albums, EPs, singles, and publishers with powerful filtering, search, and a full-featured audio player
- **Support artists with Lightning payments** - Value4Value (V4V) integration lets you send Bitcoin Lightning payments directly to artists through boost buttons
- **Save and organize your favorites** - Favorite tracks, albums, and publishers, then access them all from one place, with optional cross-device syncing via Nostr
- **Explore curated playlists** - Multiple curated playlists (HGH, ITDV, MMM, SAS, IAM, MMT, B4TS, Upbeats, Flowgnar) in Podcasting 2.0 musicL format
- **Works like a native app** - Install as PWA on iOS/Android, enjoy offline playback, background audio, and keyboard shortcuts

- **Site Basic - Playing Music:** Homepage navigation, filtering, grid/list views, album cards, infinite scroll, album/publisher pages, search, Now Playing bar, fullscreen player, play controls, progress/volume, background playback, shuffle, favorites button & page (Albums/Tracks/Publishers/Playlists tabs), sorting, settings, PWA installation, offline functionality, keyboard shortcuts, URL sharing

- **Other Highlights:** Publisher pages, grid/list view options, infinite scroll, background playback, shuffle all tracks
